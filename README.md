## Overview
Hungry Dinosaur is a simple Scratch game where you help a dinosaur eat healthy food while avoiding a dangerous ladybug.  
The project focuses on learning core programming ideas through a playful, visual experience.

## How to Play
- Use the **Left Arrow** and **Right Arrow** keys to move the dinosaur  
- Eat healthy food to increase your score  
- Avoid the ladybug â€” touching it will decrease your score

## Win / Lose Conditions
- Reach a score of **30** to win  
- If your score becomes negative, the game is over
